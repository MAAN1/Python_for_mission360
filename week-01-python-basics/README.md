ACTIVITY 1: Make a Character Move (20 minutes)
Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Create Your Scratch Account & Open Project

Go to https://scratch.mit.edu/
Click "Sign Up" (top right)
Enter username, password, email
Click "Create" to start a new project
You'll see a blank stage with a cat sprite
Step 2: Understand the Three Main Areas

Left Panel (Blocks): Colorful instruction blocks organized by category
Middle (Code Area): Where you drag blocks to build your program
Right (Stage): Where your sprite performs the actions
Step 3: Start Your Program with an Event Block

Click the red "Events" category on the left
Find "When Green Flag Clicked" block
Drag it to the middle code area
This tells the program: "Start when someone clicks the green flag"
Step 4: Make Your Sprite Move

Click the blue "Motion" category
Drag "Move 10 Steps" block below the green flag block
Click the number "10" and change it to "50"
Your blocks should now be stacked together
Step 5: Add More Movement

Drag another "Move 50 Steps" block below the first one
Click "Turn 15 Degrees" block and drag it below
Change "15" to "90"
Drag another "Move 50 Steps" block
Step 6: Make Your Sprite Talk

Click the purple "Looks" category
Find "Say Hello for 2 Seconds" block
Drag it to the bottom of your stack
Click "Hello" and change it to "I'm a Data Scientist!"
Step 7: Test Your Program

Click the green flag (top right of stage)
